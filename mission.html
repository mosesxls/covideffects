Sample Size

Convinience Sampling

Convenience sampling was also used because the Market is nearer, the technique doesn’t require planning and also no transport costs involved. We simply used participants who are available at the moment.

Purposive Sampling

According to this method, which belongs to the category of non-probability sampling techniques, sample members are selected on the basis of their knowledge, relationships and expertise regarding a research subject (Freedman et al., 2007). In this study, the sample members who were selected had special relationship with the phenomenon under investigation, sufficient and relevant experience with selling of goods during the pandemic.
